Prediction of Fetal Problems by Cardiotocography

School project on the valorization of data from a cardiotocograph to predict fetal problems. This is a multi-objective classification problem. The project presents the resolution of the trial with the approach specific to data science. Initially, we will use TPOT (Tree-Based Pipeline Optimization Tool), an AutoML tool to create a baseline that we will attempt to surpass. The experimentation also shows a test of coupling between the models adjusted on each of the target columns where the prediction of one adjusted on the first target becomes the feature of the next adjusted on the second, etc.
