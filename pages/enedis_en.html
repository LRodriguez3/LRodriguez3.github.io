Enedis

Enedis is the manager of the electricity distribution network in France. It plays a key role in the management and modernization of the electrical network, ensuring the balance between electricity supply and demand. Flexibility mechanisms are essential for adapting electricity consumption and production in real time, thus optimizing the use of energy resources.

Projects at Enedis

Panel Method

This project required the creation of a calculation engine for predicting consumption when consumption reductions have occurred. The method is already used to value flexibilities within the EIF and Flex Locale mechanisms and is expected to come into force on September 1, 2025, for NEBEF. The scope is LV below 36 kVA.

KPPVGeo

KPPVGeo is a project involving the development of a calculation engine for predicting the production of photovoltaic and wind producers on LV below 36 kVA and MV networks when they have experienced production curtailments.

NLP Data Valorization for Consumption

This project involves using data from social media posts, newspapers, or other sources via methods such as scraping, API access, or data providers to create relevant features to explain and predict consumption.
