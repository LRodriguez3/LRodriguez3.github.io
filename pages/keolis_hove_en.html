Keolis / Hove

Hove is the digital subsidiary of the Keolis group, divided into two product lines. There is first the one around Navitia, a passenger information platform and a multimodal passenger route allocation engine, followed by the Pattern team. Pattern is a team of Data People providing fixed-price services to its clients, notably Keolis, its major account client. Pattern conducts mobility studies on territories. It is within this team that I evolved and was in charge of various subjects.
